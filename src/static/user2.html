FAQ

How does installation work?

We mount a small, low‑power sensor at breathing‑zone height. It takes ~15 minutes and runs on wall power or USB.

What if my internet is weak?

The device buffers data offline and forwards when connectivity returns. It supports Wi‑Fi, 4G, and SMS/USSD for basic alerts.

What is the EatSafe score?

A composite of PM peaks, combustion gas load (CO/NO₂/VOCs), and crowding intensity inferred from CO₂, smoothed to ignore short spikes.

Do customers see my rating?

Yes. High scores get badges and visibility boosts on the public app, helping customers choose safer dining times and venues.
